About Us

Flanba Network, a unique Minecraft Bedrock server...


Our team at Flanba consists of 6 employees and many volunteers. Development started at the end of December 2021, the server is made by players for players.


Our main goal at Flanba is to create a fun server where the community can play and have fun with their friends and families and meet new people. We want to offer our players the best possible experience, so we are constantly developing new updates, adding new maps and pushing new content to the server.


This server would not be possible without our investors, many thanks to Salad.com, WitherHosting.com and our parent company sicromgmt.com!
